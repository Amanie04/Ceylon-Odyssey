Heritage & Cultural Activities


Handlooms and Batik Industry

. The Batik industry in Sri Lanka is a small scale industry which can employ individual design talent and creativity


Kandyan Dancing

Kandyan dance encompasses various dance forms popular and native to the area called Kandy of the Central Hills region known as Udarata in Sri Lanka


Ambalangoda Masks

The art of Sri Lankan Masks was existing from time immemorial. Mask carving is a local tradition in the southern coastal region in Sri Lanka.


Gems

Especially notable are blue sapphire, pink sapphire or ruby, and yellow sapphire; alexandrite and cat's-eye chrysoberyl; and almandine and hessonite garnet.


Esala Perahera Kandy

The Kandy Esala Perahera also known as The Festival of the Tooth is a festival held in August in Kandy, Sri Lanka


Sinharaja Rain Forest

Sinharaja Forest is a forest reserve and a biodiversity hotspot in Sri Lanka. It is international significance and World Heritage Site by UNESCO.
